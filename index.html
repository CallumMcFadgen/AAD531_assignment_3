About me

Hi there, I'm Callum McFadgen a Full-stack Web Developer

My partner and I have lived and worked in sunny Nelson in New Zealand’s South Island for over 10 years. In 2020, I graduated from Nelson Marlborough Institute of Technology with a bachelor’s degree in information technology majoring in Software Development and a strong interest in Full-Stack Development.

Prior to studying and working in information technology, I had worked in a range of customer focused hospitality and retail roles which provided me with excellent communication skills and a very practical end user focus in addition to my more technical skills.

Since graduating, I have worked with Nelson based ecommerce provider Airsquare Ltd in the design and development of several integrated mobile, tablet and web applications.

I take great satisfaction in designing and creating software that is practical and user friendly and I am very passionate about software adding to users buisnesses and lifestyles. I am always interested in meeting people and hearing ideas, so If you would like to get in touch, please send me a message via the contact form below and I will respond withing 2 working days.
